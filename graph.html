Graph [ Data Structures & Algorithms ]

A graph is a non-linear data structure consisting of nodes (vertices) and edges (connections) between the nodes. Graphs are widely used to represent various real-world problems such as networks, social media, and routing problems.

  • Types of Graphs:
    • Directed Graphs (Edges have a direction)
    • Undirected Graphs (Edges have no direction)
    • Weighted Graphs (Edges have weights or costs)
    • Unweighted Graphs (Edges have no weights)
    • Complete Graphs (Every pair of vertices is connected)
